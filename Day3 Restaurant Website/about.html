About Us

Hamro Newari Kitchen is a restaurant that serves traditional Newari cuisine. We are committed to providing our customers with authentic flavors and a memorable dining experience. Our menu features a variety of dishes that showcase the rich culinary heritage of the Newar community. Inspired by family recipes passed down through generations, our menu combines traditional flavors with modern touches. Every dish is prepared daily using locally sourced ingredients to ensure freshness and exceptional taste. Whether you're a local or a visitor, we invite you to join us and savor the delicious tastes of Newari food.

Our Mission

Our mission is to preserve and promote the rich culinary heritage of the Newar community while providing an exceptional dining experience for our customers. We strive to create a welcoming and authentic atmosphere where guests can enjoy traditional Newari cuisine made with love and care. We are dedicated to using high-quality ingredients, supporting local farmers, and continuously innovating our menu to delight our customers with every visit.

Our Values

  1. Quality ingredients
  2. Authentic recipes
  3. Cleanliness
  4. Sustainability
  5. Friendly service
  6. Community support

Our Team

Our team is made up of passionate individuals who are dedicated to providing an exceptional dining experience for our customers. From our skilled chefs who craft each dish with care to our friendly servers who ensure that every guest feels welcome, we work together to create a memorable experience for everyone who walks through our doors. We are proud of our team and their commitment to excellence in everything they do.
