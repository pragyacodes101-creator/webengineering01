Menu

Our menu features a variety of dishes that showcase the rich culinary heritage of the Newar community. Inspired by family recipes passed down through generations, our menu combines traditional flavors with modern touches. Every dish is prepared daily using locally sourced ingredients to ensure freshness and exceptional taste. Whether you're a local or a visitor, we invite you to join us and savor the delicious tastes of Newari food.
